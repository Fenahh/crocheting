Crotchet

What is to crochet?

Crochet is a craft technique that involves using a hooked needle to create fabric by interlocking loops of yarn or thread, but instead of using two needles like in knitting, crochet uses a single hook and is typically faster. In my opinion, its either art or a form of magic. Its like painting with strands, turning a blank canvas into a warm tapestry of stitches, creating a symphony of textures with each loop only using a single piece of metal and some sort of magical yarn! It can be used to make anything from hats to blankets, sweaters, socks, and even plushies. There's practically no limit, so let your imagination run wild.

Crocheting is a great way to let loose, relax and take your mind off things. It is personally one of my most interesting and enjoyable hobbies but many people make a business out of it. I have so much respect for them because I am a victim of what I call "dozens of unfinished projects syndrome".

If you have ever seen someone crocheting in public (or in my case, on tiktok) and wondered what exactly it was? Or maybe you've tried to crochet before, but just didn't know what you were doing? I'm gonna give you a step by step guide to where you can start and a few resources as well!
